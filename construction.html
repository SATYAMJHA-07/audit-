CODE OF CONDUCT:

• Provide an oversight to the company compliance and company conducted program. Review the program and policies of the company in order to ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations and monitor the result of such compliance efforts. Require management to report on procedures that provide assurance that the company’s mission and values, as enumerated in its business strategy plan are properly communicated to all employees. Encourage the employees to reflect the company’s mission and values in their decision and actions in order to convey it to all employees’ acceptable business conduct in practice.
