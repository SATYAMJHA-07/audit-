Roles and Responsibility of Risk Officers:

• Audit plan

Internal Audit Head is supposed to prepare a quarterly manual audit plan. The plan discloses how the department will go about carrying the audit exercise. It tells which branch will be audited, when, resource allocation, number of days of audit and tentative date of submission of report. Audit plan is a confidential document and shared only with the MD of the organization.

•Sampling

As it is not possible to check all transactions or meet all clients, the internal audit can be done on sampling basis. Auditors have to sample out the transactions, records and reports to be checked and clients to be visited. While sampling, following things have to be considered

•The sample should be representative of entire portfolio and should be able to cover all the geographic locations and all the field staff;

•The sample should put more emphasis on the vulnerable areas like cash handling, groups having repayment problems;

•Different samples can be obtained for different aspects like 50% of all the loan card of visited JLG will be check, 100% entries in the cash book will be checked, 2% of the borrowers will be visited, etc...

•Audit report and Grading

The audit of branch includes different parameters which will be considered during the branch audit. The result of audit is defined in terms of grading which will be depended on the scores and scores shall be given by auditor on the basis of the fulfilment of various compliances as per the policies and process of the company and these scores shall be defined on the basis of different parameters.

...........................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................

•SCORING

During the audit, auditor must check all the compliances as per the company’s policies which a branch needs to comply and different score will be given for operation, accounts, IT, legal etc... The scoring distribution for the audit is as mentioned below:

•Operations Score=40,

•Accounts & finance Score=4,

•IT Score =10,

•Legal Documentation Score=6,

•Office Documentation Score=40,

•Branch total Score=100,

...........................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................

•GRADING

When a branch is audited, the result of audit is given in term of grading. There are four types of grading are given and these grading are depends on the number of scores obtained in the audit as mentioned below:

•85 &Above A

•75 to 84 B

•65 to 74 C

•below 64 D

•Compliance

The audit report is presented to the Directors, if required. The report must be considered by the middle management and it is the responsibility of the middle management to see that the findings are followed up properly and required actions are initiated. Branch Manager shall consider the shortcomings in the report and improve them. The auditors in the next auditing must verify if the shortcomings reported in last report have been addressed or not. If not, then it should be mentioned in report that no action has been taken despite mention in audit report last time. Branch audit will be conducted on quarterly Basis, after compilation of Branch audit Risk officers send audit Report to the Respective BM by marking copy to concerned Area Manager, Division manager, Team Leader and Audit manager Also.

After rectification of audit findings, a hard copy of Compliance certificate will be given to the respective Branch manager. This certificate insures that all the audit findings pertaining to the previous month has been rectified and checked by the audit team.

Compliance on High risk issues raised by Audit manager must be closed by the respective Divisional Manager/Area manager within a week of escalation. If it is not done within time, then same will be escalated to next level (Business Head) and all such pending issues are also raised at the audit committee.
